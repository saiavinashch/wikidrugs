'''Vedaprofen''' is a [[nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug]] (NSAID) used in [[veterinary medicine]] for the treatment of pain and inflammation due to musculoskeletal disorders in dogs and horses and for the treatment of pain due to [[horse colic]].<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.merckvetmanual.com/mvm/index.jsp?cfile=htm/bc/191606.htm | publisher = Merck Sharp & Dohme Corp | work = Merck Veterinary Manual | title = Specific Nonsteroidal Anti-inflammatory Drugs}}</ref>
